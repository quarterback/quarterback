## Running

- **[occupant.ee](https://occupant.ee)** — Public benchmarks for AI compute. Three daily indices: Compute CPI (price deflation across model tiers), AEAI (token throughput, spend, energy), LDI (labor displacement cost-per-workload). 2,300+ models, open methodology.
- **[oregontennis.org](https://oregontennis.org)** — The only power ranking system for Oregon high school tennis.
- **[makingpublicwork.com](https://makingpublicwork.com)** — Newsletter. Essays on how services actually work.

## Tinkering

- **[heypbj.xyz](https://heypbj.xyz)** — Dating app built on Stratified Preference Allocation. 15 tiered slots per week instead of infinite swipes. Testing whether scarcity plus legibility produces different outcomes than the Big Three.
- **[leafroll.fun](https://leafroll.fun)** — Professional network where connections cost credits and both sides review each other. Argument: LinkedIn rewards collecting, not knowing.
- **[The Chip Window](https://gamedesign.leaflet.pub/3mjkhluucpc2j)** — NBA draft reform mechanism that severs the tie between losing and draft position. Bid Standardization replaces the lottery: teams wager chips over a 22-game window, most chips picks first. Live simulation: [giantplanet.org](https://giantplanet.org/).
- **[Viperball](https://github.com/quarterback/viperball)** — Collegiate dynasty simulator for a sport that doesn't exist. Started as a Reddit thread about 1894 Yale vs. a modern team, became a full rulebook: no forward pass, six downs, drop kicks for 5, 187-team women's league. The sport is the prototype.
- **[kizukizu.club](https://kizukizu.club)** — Relationship compatibility assessment built on seven Nordic-inspired archetypes. 33 questions across five modules. Surfaces how your inner landscape interacts with someone else's, not which "type" you are.
